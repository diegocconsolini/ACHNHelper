How It Works

Default (Panel Closed)

  • Grid takes full width
  • Compact bottom bar shows selected cell info
  • Click "Panel ▶" or double-click a cell to open panel

Panel Open

  • Panel slides in from right (~250px)
  • Grid shrinks but stays fully usable
  • 3 tabs: Breed (pairs + offspring), Sim (day simulation), Save (layouts)
  • Click "◀ Close" or press Escape to collapse
